The Best Movies According to Kemal Bilge

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Clockwork Orange

This a a movie of Stanley Kubrick who is one of my favourite director and this movie discuss the goodness and badness of human being and how to approach this philosophically.

Boyhood

This movie is directed by David Linklater and has been shoten in 11 years. It is a story about growing of a boy and this long filming process planned intentionally to include exactly the growth of the actor in film.

Once Upon a Time in Anatolia

This is a movie of Nuri Bilge Ceylan directed in 2011 and won Palme Dor. This movie is a good analyse of the people of Anatolia where I was born and grow up and for this reason I really like it.
